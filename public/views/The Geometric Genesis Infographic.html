Objective 3: Quantum Properties as Topology

This objective models fundamental properties like spin and entanglement as emergent features of the hypergraph's topology.

Entanglement as Geometric Duality

Entangled particles are modeled as a perfectly dual pair of nodes, like the vertices of a cube and the faces of its dual, the octahedron. Observing one instantly defines the other due to their inseparable geometric definition.
